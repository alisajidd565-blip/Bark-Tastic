Meet Your Groomer

Hi, I'm Danielle — and I've Been Doing This Since 2005

I started my career at PetSmart in California as a dog trainer and pet stylist. By the time I left, I was managing the entire pet salon. After years of working across California and Georgia, I brought my skills home to Ottumwa, Iowa — where I opened Bark-Tastic Pawlor LLC to serve our community.

Before going out on my own, I spent two years at Paws'a'tively Canine Creations sharpening my grooming craft. Now every appointment is handled personally, by me — no junior staff, no shortcuts. Your pet gets my full attention every single time.

I'm also a Licensed Practical Nurse, which means I'm trained to notice things other groomers might miss — like skin conditions, lumps, or ear issues. I'll always flag anything that looks off.
